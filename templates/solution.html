    • What can we do?



    Like all problems, this one also has a solution.
    Some ways we as humans can fix overpopulation include:

    - Manage resources sustainably (don't waste paper)
    - Save water
    - Reduce food waste

    This way, we can ensure that future population growth will not lead to a bad quality of life for future generations.
    However, there are also other things we can get governments to do:

    - Set better agricultural standards (use more efficient farming techniques)
    - Shift more funds on infrastructural research
    - Set better housing standards (lesser slums)
    - Use land more efficiently (build up instead of out)
