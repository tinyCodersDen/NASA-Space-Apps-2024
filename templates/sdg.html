    • The 11th UN SDG



    To make cities inclusive, safe, resilient and sustainable



    The 11th SDG, Sustainable Cities and Communities, is the most prevalent SDG in overpopulation.
    In order to fix overpopulation issues, we must create sustainable communities that can maintain enough people.
    The 11th UN SDG can also help us visualize the NASA Data and see how city populations have increased over time.

    If you want to see how the graphs were made, click on one of the images.

    Making sustainable cities and communities can help mitigate the impacts of overpopulation.
    It can also serve as a solution to develop communities to maintain future population growth.
    As you can see, the 11th UN SDG can help various cities across the world to fix overpopulation.
